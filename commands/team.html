Command Name Description Usage
!team teamname Shows information about a specific Overwatch League team !team teamname [accounts|schedule]

Team Info

Look up specific information about your favorite Overwatch League teams! You can see their current league standing, match records, website, division, and roster. Each team has additional commands for viewing their social media accounts, and match schedule for the current stage.















Team Accounts

Access an Overwatch League team's account list with the optional account argument. Teams can be on various platforms including Twitch, YouTube, Discord, Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, and Weibo.













Team Schedule

View a team's upcoming match schedule for the current stage, and previous matches with the optional schedule argument.
